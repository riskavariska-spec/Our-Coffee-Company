Our Story in Every Bean

At Kava Nusa, every cup tells a story of islands, rain, and volcanic earth. We partner directly with smallholder farmers across Sumatra, Java, Bali, Sulawesi, Flores, and Papua to bring you authentic coffees — ethically sourced, small-batch roasted, and delivered fresh to Türkiye.

We prepare and load coffee in Yogyakarta City, export through Semarang Port (Tanjung Emas), and distribute locally from Bursa, Türkiye. This setup gives Turkish buyers fast access and reliable support.

Export & Shipping

We manage every step for a smooth supply chain between Indonesia and Türkiye.

Sourcing & Loading

Coffee is collected and prepared in Yogyakarta City. Small-batch roasting available there or in Bursa on request.

Export Point

Shipments depart through Semarang Port (Tanjung Emas) — well-connected for Central Java exports.

Distribution

Arrives in Türkiye and is distributed from Bursa to Istanbul, Izmir, Mersin, and other cities.

Logistics & Terms

  • Export Terms: FOB (Semarang) / CIF available to Türkiye ports
  • Packaging: 60–70 kg jute bags or GrainPro-lined bags for green coffee
  • Documents: Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, Quality Report
  • Estimated transit: 25–30 days (sea freight)
